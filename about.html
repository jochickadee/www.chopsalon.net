About Chop Salon

"Chop Salon opened it's doors in July of 2009. It's located in the beautiful North Berkeley Area just blocks from the gourmet ghetto and the North Berkeley BART station. We have plenty of free street parking. All of our stylists have continued with education to keep with trends and to strengthen our skills to give the best possible service for all of our clients. We hope to see you for your next hair appointment!"
